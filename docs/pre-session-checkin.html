Pre-session check-in

Use this before the first session to understand access, comfort, participation preferences, and consent boundaries. It is intentionally not a form: print it, screenshot it, or adapt the questions into your own approved survey system.

No responses are stored by this page.

Facilitator note. Treat non-use of AI tools as a normal participation pathway, not as an exception to manage. The goal is to learn what supports people need before they are asked to perform in front of others.

01Access and device readiness

What device and internet access will you likely have during the sessions?

Reliable laptop or desktop
Phone or tablet only
Uncertain or shared access

02Account and tool constraints

Are there account, age, district, workplace, payment, or policy limits that affect your use of AI tools?

03Participation pathway

Which starting pathway feels most workable for you right now?

Use AI tools directly
Observe or critique examples
Critical / No-AI pathway

04Comfort with sharing work

What kinds of work are you comfortable sharing with the group, and what should remain private?

Share in full group
Share anonymously
Do not share without asking

05Learning goals

What do you most want to understand, make, critique, or adapt by the end of the workshop?

06Support needs

What would help you participate well: pacing, captions, alternatives to live generation, small-group options, examples in advance, or something else?

07Consent boundaries

Are there topics, materials, images, names, classroom artifacts, or participant work that should not be pasted into AI tools or displayed publicly?
