Session 2 · Images

Frozen image prompt families

Use these examples to discuss visual defaults, missing context, and revision choices when real media is not available.

Professional default

A portrait of a software engineer presenting a new machine learning tool to a classroom.
Observed defaultLikely to overrepresent polished tech-demo aesthetics and narrow ideas of expertise.
Revision triedAdd age range, role diversity, classroom context, and what the audience is doing.
Ethical concernProfessional imagery can encode who appears to belong in technical authority.

Community place

A public library workshop where people are learning how image generators make pictures.
Observed defaultThe model may invent generic library markers while missing local culture and access needs.
Revision triedSpecify wheelchair access, multilingual signage, facilitators, and non-laptop participation.
Ethical concernCommunity images can flatten real places into stock-like scenes.

Scientific diagram

A clear diagram showing how noise becomes an image during diffusion.
Observed defaultDiagrams often imply a clean left-to-right pipeline and hide training data.
Revision triedAdd labels for training examples, denoising step, and human prompt choice.
Ethical concernSimplified diagrams can make extraction and uncertainty disappear.
