Two screens, one deck

A private cockpit for you, a clean slide for the room — the live tools sync between them, and everything survives a refresh.

You see — presenter

The slide preview, your script, what the room does, the timer, and the controls for each live tool.

They see — audience

Only the slide: the big idea, the room's task, and the live tool's visual. No notes, no controls.

  1. Click Open audience window. A clean slide opens in a new window.
  2. In Zoom, Share Screen → that window (not your whole screen).
  3. Keep this cockpit private. Move with ← →; tally, sample, and clicks all mirror to the shared slide.
  4. The sentence, tally, points, and scores persist across refreshes. Use new session in the top bar for a clean run, and copy log after to export what the room built.
