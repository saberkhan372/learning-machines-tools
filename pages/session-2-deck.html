Revised after the Session 1 run. A private cockpit for you, a clean slide for the room; the live tools sync between them and survive a refresh.

The slide preview, your script, what the room does, the timer, and the controls for each live tool.

They see — audience

Only the slide: the big idea, the room's task, and the live tool's visual. No notes, no controls.
