New to AI investigation?

How these tools were made

AI use in development

These tools were designed and built with AI assistance (Claude, Anthropic) throughout — code generation, content drafting, facilitation scripts, and iteration. All pedagogical framing, session design, and tool concepts were authored by human educators and reviewed against classroom use. Every facilitation guide was written for real sessions, not generated and published unchanged.

Classroom origins

Investigation activities, bridge framings, and discussion questions draw on Generative AI as a Creative Collaborator, a UC-approved high school elective co-taught by Saber Khan and Danny Gámez at Campbell Hall School, Los Angeles. Student testing and classroom iteration shaped these tools directly.

CC Fest context

Learning Machines is a CC Fest program. CC Fest builds free, open creative technology education for educators, artists, and learners. This camp extends the CC Fest Coding Camp into AI literacy and critical investigation. Source is open on GitHub.

Static HTML, vanilla JavaScript, no required accounts, and no live model dependency for core teaching moments.
